Robustness of entered network

The network centrality measure helps identifying the central nodes based on their position in the supply chain. The importance of a node can be evaluated using centrality measures like degree. The tool uses sequential High Degree Centrality Attack (HDA) attack strategy for calculating robustness.

{{robustnessHDA}}

In random attack, the nodes are removed in randomfashion till N-1 nodes, where N represents the totalnodes in the supply chain network.
{{robustnessRandom}}

To calculate the robustness of the entered supply chain, every disruption event is considered. The provided robustness value os the mean of all the robustness values obtained.
